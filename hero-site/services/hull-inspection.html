Hull & Running
Gear Inspection

Comprehensive diver-performed visual inspection and documentation of vessel hulls and all running gear components.

What We Inspect

Every Inch. Below the Waterline.

Our certified dive professionals perform thorough visual inspections of your vessel's underwater components. Every dive produces organized photo and video documentation with a structured condition summary suitable for insurance review and maintenance planning.

🔍

Hull Surface

Full inspection of hull plating, bottom paint condition, osmotic blistering, impact damage, and marine growth assessment.

⚙️

Shafts & Props

Propeller blade condition, shaft alignment indicators, cutlass bearing play, and zinc anode deterioration levels.

🧭

Rudders & Struts

Rudder bearing play, strut mounting integrity, and all appendage fastener inspection with corrosion documentation.

🌊

Thru-Hulls & Intakes

Every below-waterline penetration documented. Valve condition, hose clamp integrity, and marine growth obstruction assessment.

The Process

How It Works

01

Schedule & Brief

Contact us with your vessel location and requirements. We'll confirm scheduling, access logistics, and any specific areas of concern you want prioritized.

02

Dive & Document

Our certified diver enters the water and performs a systematic inspection. Every component is photographed and recorded on video with narrated observations.

03

Report Delivery

You receive a structured condition report with organized media, component-by-component findings, and actionable maintenance recommendations. Insurance-ready format.
